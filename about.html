About Us

Welcome to BamAcon Real Estate Company, a fully licensed firm in Addis Ababa. At BamAcon Real Estate, we believe that your journey in real estate transcends mere transactions; it's an immersive experience tailored for your success. Whether you're purchasing a home or making an investment, our committed team is here to provide personalized attention and expert guidance throughout the entire process

Vision
To redefine the industry with unparalleled support, integrity, and excellence, aiming to lead in East Africa.
Mission
Empower individuals and families to achieve real estate goals with exceptional service and personalized solutions.
Core Value
At Bamacon, we uphold the core values of unwavering integrity in all our dealings. Ethical practice, dependability, responsibility, punctuality and accuracy guide our every action, ensuring trust and transparency with our clients and partners.

Message from the CEO

Welcome to our real estate company. With over 20 years of experience in the construction industry and a dedicated focus on real estate development for the past 5 years, we are proud to offer premier locations across Addis Ababa.

Our projects are strategically located in the most sought-after areas of the city, ensuring easy access to essential services, transportation, and vibrant community life. This prime positioning not only enhances the living experience for our residents but also maximizes the value and return on investment for our buyers.

We provide well-designed and comfortable apartments, business areas, offices, and shops, complemented by gymnasium facilities and related amenities. These features ensure our residents and users enjoy a high-quality lifestyle and entertainment options.

Our commitment to fast project delivery means that investors can expect quicker returns on their investments. By leveraging cutting-edge technology in construction and design, we achieve efficiency and excellence. Our sister companies, specializing in post-tensioning, pile foundations, and construction material production, strengthen our capability to deliver projects quickly, economically, and with exceptional quality.

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to our valued buyers for your continuous investment in our properties. Your trust and support motivate us to consistently exceed expectations and deliver superior value.

Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Eng Girma Gelaw 

Chief Executive Officer
